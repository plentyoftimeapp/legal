Privacy Policy — Plenty of Time

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Plenty of Time is built to know as little about you as possible.

What we collect: nothing. The app has no accounts, no sign-up, and no analytics. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our own servers, because we don't have any servers.

Your location is used on your device to work out journey times from where you are. It is sent to Google's mapping services (along with your searched destinations) solely to calculate routes, traffic, and parking. It is never stored by us or linked to your identity. See Google's privacy policy for how Google processes mapping requests.

Train trips query National Rail live departure information. No personal data is included in those requests.

Your trips and saved places are stored only on your device. Deleting the app deletes them.

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

Children: the app is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone.

Changes: if this policy changes, the date above changes with it.
